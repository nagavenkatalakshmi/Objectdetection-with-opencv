About This Project


Imagine a scenario in a warehouse where quick and accurate inventory counts are crucial. ObjectQuantify addresses this need by allowing users to upload or capture images containing various objects in stock. The system then automatically identifies and quantifies these objects by drawing bounding boxes around them, making the inventory management process seamless and error-free.

User-Friendly Interface:

Offers a simple and intuitive interface for users to upload images, making the process accessible even for those new to computer vision.

Practical Applications:

Suitable for a wide range of applications, including inventory management, quality control, and other scenarios requiring object counting

Efficient Object Detection:

Utilizes OpenCV and cvlib for robust object detection, ensuring efficient identification of objects within images
